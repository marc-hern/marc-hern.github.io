Profit Success Prediction in IMDB Dataset
This notebook is intended to serve two purposes:

  • To generally explore the initial dataset
  • To discover any correlation between the dataset features and the success of a movie

The scope of this project includes only movies made in the United States and I have defined a movie to be a "success" if it made any profit regardless of amount.

Used: Python, IPython Notebook, KNN, and logistic regression.

A competition sponsored by NASA involving the optimization of fully autonomous rovers in completing specialized tasks.

Rovers were to identify objects with special tags, pick them up and deliver them to a home base, and repeat the entire process.

Used: C++, Tensorflow, Gazebo.
